World NGO Day

World NGO Day, observed annually on 27 February, was recognised on 17 April 2010 by 12 countries of the IX Baltic Sea NGO Forum at the eighth Summit of the Baltic Sea States in Vilnius, Lithuania. It was internationally recognised on 28 February 2014 in Helsinki, Finland by United Nations Development Programme administrator and former Prime Minister of New Zealand Helen Clark.

Integrated Rural Development & Achievements

We are also continually working on financial literacy campaign in the districts of Uttarakhand to enhance and promote better livelihood.
In livelihood, we have emphasized modern techniques of agriculture and horticulture, Organic agriculture, cash crops and others. We have collaborated with NABARD, Banks and other financial institutions for business startup initiatives.
Sanjeevani also works on livelihood option for vulnerable section and persons with disabilities. We have formed 370 community based organization in the Uttarakhand. We also have formed 250 SHG group members for livelihood and small business activities. We have catered the livelihood initiatives to 15000 Families in various trades of farm & non-farm activities.
Organization imparted 220 Sustainable Livelihood Trainings on various trades of farm and non-farm trades. 4976number of persons have started to earn their livelihood Income.
Out of which 257 have become Master Trainers and executing their livelihood and working as business entity and at the same time they have started imparting training to others.
135 Master trainers have become Resource Person& imparted training in their area and associated with other programmes also.
We have gone extra mile to expand our horizon by forming 2 Farmers Producers Organizations. Now Farmers have come under formed FPOs umbrella. Farmers have started their sale through FPOs. These new borne FPOs will work as business entity very soon in upcoming time.
We have converged government schemes and services worth of INR 5.60 crore around covering all our projects and particularly livelihood and integrated rural development.
Afforestation: A huge plantation has done under Oak Tasar program. The program is being implemented for the upliftment and livelihood creation of tribal families in Bageshwar district.
